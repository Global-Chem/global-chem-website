Open Source Governance

Global-Chem is a record of what is common in the world of all molecules for all different communities of people. It is a dictionary of common names that humans use for example with the molecule written directly, for example Vitamin C is:

C(C(C1C(=C(C(=O)O1)O)O)O)O

The molecular structure is not ambigious to us the people as long as we keep an accurate record of what names are corresponding to what molecule. We have a hard time understanding what is actually inside of the products or things we use commonly and it is affecting our health and our planet. We need to organize our common knowledge together into an organized global knowledge graph and learn how all these molecules relate to each other. What is in your Food? What inactive ingredients are in your Medicines? Sex, Cannabis, War, the lists of chemical products is infinite we need to understand how our world works as a community of people and have the molecules that we use in modern society governed by us, the open source community. Global-Chem is the first Open Source Knowledge Graph of Chemical List Governed by the People.
